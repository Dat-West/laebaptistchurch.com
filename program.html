Program

Here in Lae Baptist Church, we have two catergories of Programs. One is our church weekly program which runs consistantly every week with a goal to keep the church unified and active in our spiritual lives, and the other is our anuall events that take place in our main church in East Taraka Lae or one of our churches in Morobe Province purposely for church growth and revival.
