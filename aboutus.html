About Us

Welcome to Lae Baptist Church! We are a vibrant and welcoming community of believers located in the heart of Morobe. Our mission is to glorify God through worship, discipleship, and service, and to share the love of Jesus Christ with our neighbors and the world. Founded in 1972, Lae Baptist Church has been a cornerstone of faith and fellowship in our community. Over the years, we have grown from a small gathering of believers to a thriving congregation committed to living out the teachings of the Bible. Our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ by teaching the Word of God, fostering spiritual growth, and serving our community with love and compassion. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of fellowship, and the call to evangelize. We hold to the core beliefs of the Christian faith, including the Trinity, the divinity of Jesus Christ, and the authority of the Scriptures. We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ, and that we are called to live lives that reflect His love and grace. At Lae Baptist Church, we are more than just a congregation; we are a family. We offer a variety of ministries and programs for all ages, including Sunday School, youth groups, Bible studies, and outreach initiatives. Whether you are new to the area or looking for a church home, we invite you to join us and experience the warmth and fellowship of our community. We welcome you to join us for worship services every Sunday at 9:30 am and for our beginning of the week Prayer Night on Monday at 6:30 pm. Come as you are and discover a place where you can grow in your faith, build meaningful relationships, and make a difference in the world.

Life Testimony

I was born and raised up in a Christian family. However, as I grew up to my youth, completed my High School Education, and started working in the city and earning salary, I was greatly influenced by the corrupted system of this fallen world. I truly sinned against myself and against God through my bad conducts and actions. My life testimony was so distorted and tarnished for doing evil and Satanic activities. One day, after taking alcohol or beer after work on Friday, I led a group of drunkard people and we went around in Lae city looking for dance. I still remembered that moment, as a Professional Salesman of the company, I have a lot of money. We were looking for dance everywhere and we ended up dancing in a Crusade hosted at the Eriku Oval. That night, I surrendered my life to the Lord Jesus Christ. As I stood among the many who gave their lives to God that night, I heard a whispering voice saying, “Morris, you are a one of those professional salesmen who always goes out and talks, sells, and convinces a lot of customers to buy your company’s product. Today I will change that technique around for my kingdom’s sake. I will use you – I will send you out to sell my product, that is to preach my good news and bring more profit into my kingdom.” After hearing that soft voice, I went straight to the company the next day and turned in my resignation. From there on, as I continue on my walk with the Lord, praying and fasting, and showing my hunger and thirst to God for His Word, the rest are history. I am Evangelist Morris B. Kanom, one of God’s Professional Salesmen, hallelujah!


Number Of Years in Evangelistic, Teaching, and Preaching Ministry

I have been serving God in the ministry of preaching, teaching, and evangelizing for about a total of 17 years. I am serving God still.



View of Ministry

While serving God in Ministry during these 17 years, I came to realize that, what God said during the time I surrendered my life to Him, has come to pass. In reality, I saw God working through my ministry bringing the lost souls back to Him. I have seen many lives changed and transformed when I preached, taught, and evangelized in many places during Sunday Services, Conferences, Crusades, Fellowship Nights, Men’s and Women’s retreat etc. God used me to bring more profits into His kingdom – plundering hell and populating heaven is what the Word of God and Holy Spirit does in and through my ministry. Therefore, my view of ministry stands clear, and that is to people with God.


Personal Vision

My vision is to see Christians growing up in their spiritual growth to full maturity by putting their faith in Christ Jesus. And not only that, but having an intimate relationship with Christ and walking with Him every day exhibiting discipline attitudes would enable a believer to resist Satan and his deceptive plans.


Best Encouragement with Best Scriptures

If you want to grow stronger in Christ Jesus, you must love your Bible and submit under the leading of the Holy Spirit. Showing your love for God’s Word and allowing Holy Spirit to lead you would inspire your faith, love, and hope in Christ. God gave us His Word to provide courage, peace, and hope when we most need it. You are not by yourself in facing hardships. We all face difficulties when we doubt God’s goodness and plan and need encouragement.
Therefore, do not give up! Meditate on the Word of God Day and Night and remember to keep them so that you continue to grow up strong in the Lord. (1 Thessalonians 5:11; Romans 15:13)
